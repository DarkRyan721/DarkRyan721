## 💫 About Me

I build **AI-powered backend systems** that turn operational complexity into intelligent, scalable software.

Currently working as an **AI Software Developer at AllStar Colombia**, where I design data pipelines, REST APIs, and AI-assisted decision tools for industrial-grade platforms. In parallel, I'm co-developing a **WhatsApp AI commercial assistant** targeting the Colombian B2B market — starting with the furniture sector.

My engineering background in **Mechatronics (Pontificia Universidad Javeriana)** gave me a foundation in systems thinking and numerical modeling. My thesis pushes that further: an open-source simulation platform for **Hall-effect thrusters**, combining finite-element solvers and scientific visualization for aerospace research.

I specialize at the intersection of **applied AI, backend engineering, and real-world operations**.
